About Us

Our Purpose & People

In a world driven by the urgent need for environmental preservation, The Nature Foundation is a leading non-profit organization with a mission to create, fund and promote nature conservation projects from a local to global scale. Helping both people and wildlife thru biodiversity and climate efforts, we aim to support projects in vital areas such as ocean & river pollution cleanup, reef restoration, land conservation, reforestation and carbon reduction.
