The Green Legacy Fund

With this fund, we raise resources to buy and manage essential conservation areas—ensuring no opportunity to protect biodiversity is missed. Designed to safeguard fragile ecosystems, the Forever Nature Fund enables fast action when high-priority land enters the market.This dedicated fund ensures that when land of conservation importance is up for sale, we have the means to protect it—forever.

Kids Caring for Country™

Kids on Country™ fosters confidence and wellbeing in young Aboriginal participants, offering life skills training and introducing career pathways in conservation. By learning on country, young Aboriginal people gain valuable skills, cultural strength, and a passion for caring for the land. Designed for Aboriginal youth, this program strengthens employability, builds confidence, and connects participants with country and conservation work.

Nature-Based Offset Services

Whether you're a developer needing to meet offset obligations or a landholder interested in generating income from native vegetation, we offer tailored solutions to support both. We help developers meet offset requirements and assist landholders in turning native vegetation into income through expert, on-ground solutions. From compliance to conservation, our offset services connect project developers and landowners to practical, sustainable outcomes.
